# Permissions

This reference describes the inferred roles and their capabilities within the Monad ecosystem. These roles are derived from the public structure of monad.xyz and its associated platforms.

## Roles

### Validator

Operators who run Monad nodes and participate in MonadBFT consensus. Monad supports 200+ validators.

### Protocol Team

Members of the Monad Foundation and core development team who maintain the protocol, publish announcements, and manage official channels.

### Developer

Individuals or teams building applications, smart contracts, and tooling on Monad. Developers interact with the network via EVM-compatible tooling.

### Community Member

General participants who follow announcements, attend events, join the forum, and engage with ecosystem projects.

### Institutional Partner

Organizations such as funds, exchanges, and infrastructure providers that participate in the Monad network at an institutional level.
