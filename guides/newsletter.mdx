## Overview

The Monad Memo is the official Monad newsletter, available at [monad.xyz/newsletter](https://monad.xyz/newsletter). It delivers ecosystem updates, protocol news, event announcements, and developer resources directly to subscribers.

## Subscribing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Newsletter Page">
    Navigate to [monad.xyz/newsletter](https://monad.xyz/newsletter).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Email">
    Provide your email address in the subscription form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Subscription">
    Check your inbox for a confirmation email and verify your subscription.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Content

The Monad Memo typically covers:

- Protocol updates and technical milestones
- Ecosystem project highlights
- Upcoming events and hackathons
- Developer program announcements
- Community spotlights
