## Overview

Monad provides several developer-facing resources: technical documentation at docs.monad.xyz, a developer portal at developers.monad.xyz, a hackathon platform, and a research forum. These are accessible from the Build dropdown in the navigation bar.

## Getting Started as a Developer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Documentation">
    Read the technical docs at [docs.monad.xyz](https://docs.monad.xyz) for network details, RPC methods, and contract standards.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Tooling">
    Monad supports standard Ethereum tools: Hardhat, Foundry, ethers.js, viem, and wagmi. Configure your RPC endpoint to point to the Monad network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy a Contract">
    Use the developer portal tutorials to deploy your first contract on Monad.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join the Research Forum">
    Participate in technical discussions at [forum.monad.xyz](https://forum.monad.xyz) for protocol-level questions and proposals.
  </Step>
</Steps>
