## Overview

The Monad homepage at [monad.xyz](https://monad.xyz) communicates the platform's core value proposition across several sections: performance metrics, EVM compatibility, decentralization, ecosystem access, events, and media. This guide walks through each section in order.

## Hero Section

The hero section introduces Monad with headline messaging and a call to action for developers and users.

## A New Benchmark for Crypto

This section presents Monad's positioning as a performance benchmark in the blockchain space, highlighting the combination of speed, compatibility, and decentralization.

<Frame caption="A new benchmark for crypto">![A new benchmark for crypto](/images/0002_home_benchmark.png)</Frame>

## Performance

The performance section displays Monad's key metrics: 10,000 TPS, 0.4-second block times, and 0.8-second finality. These numbers are the result of pipelined execution and optimistic parallel transaction processing.

<Frame caption="Performance metrics">![Performance metrics](/images/0003_home_performance.png)</Frame>
