## Overview

The Brand & Media Kit at [monad.xyz/brand-and-media-kit](https://monad.xyz/brand-and-media-kit) provides official Monad branding assets for use by ecosystem partners, media, and community members. It includes logos, color palettes, typography, and usage guidelines.
