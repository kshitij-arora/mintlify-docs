## Overview

The Institutions page at [monad.xyz/institutions](https://monad.xyz/institutions) presents Monad's value proposition for enterprise and institutional users. It covers four primary use cases: high-frequency finance, payments, tokenization, and agent economies.

## Institutions Hero

The hero section positions Monad as infrastructure for institutional-grade applications, emphasizing throughput and finality characteristics.
